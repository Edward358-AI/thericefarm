üîÑ Trade & Chance Rates

Better Seed Chance: When buying regular seeds, you have a chance to receive better seeds instead.

  • Base Chance: 10%
  • Decrease: -1% per 1,000 better seeds received this hour
  • Rank Bonus: +5% at Average rank or higher
  • Floor: 0%

Brown Seed Trade: Trade better seeds for brown seeds.

  • Base Cost: 20 better seeds = 1 brown seed
  • Increase: +3 per 100 brown seeds traded this hour

Gold Seed Trade: Trade brown seeds for gold seeds.

  • Base Cost: 10,000 brown seeds = 1 gold seed
  • Increase: +100 per gold seed traded this hour
üí° Expert Rank Benefit: Upon reaching Expert (Lvl 70), market prices stabilize permanently at 100% value (no fluctuations).
